## **Problem Statement**

We all know how hard it can be to plan our study time. Sometimes we spend too much time on one subject and barely touch the others. Making a timetable manually is confusing and takes a lot of time. This project helps by creating a simple, personalized study plan based on the subjects you want to study, how hard each subject is for you, and how many hours you have in a day.

## **Scope of the Project**

* You can add **any subjects** you want.
* You can tell the program how **difficult or important** each subject is.
* The program will divide your total study hours fairly, giving more time to tougher subjects.
* It has a **simple GUI** so you don’t have to use the command line.
* You get a **clear timetable** instantly showing how long to study each subject.
* It’s designed for **daily study planning**, not for long-term semester schedules.
* Works offline — no internet or extra files needed.

## **Target Users**

* Students preparing for exams.
* Anyone juggling multiple subjects who wants a structured plan.
* Students who waste time trying to figure out how long to study each subject.
* People who want a **quick and easy tool** for daily study scheduling.

## **High-Level Features**

* **Add Subjects:** Enter any subjects you want to study.
* **Set Priority:** Assign difficulty or importance to each subject (like 1–5).
* **Smart Time Allocation:** The tool divides your study hours based on difficulty.
* **Instant Output:** Get a ready-to-use timetable in seconds.
* **Simple GUI:** Easy to use window for input and output.
* **Readable Timetable:** Shows exactly how much time to spend on each subject.
